Job Resources

This is a list of websites where a candidate can search for startup jobs or a startup can search for candidates. Although some startups do list jobs on traditional Job Boards, this list includes the less traditional, but oftentimes more effective places to look for startup jobs and candidates, including Startup Accelerator Portfolios, Venture Capital Firm Portfolios, Crowdfunding Sites, Coding Competitions, and Programmer Websites.

This is content based on the book {{ site.medium_title }} by {{ site.author }}. See the "Getting a Job at a Startup" and "Hiring for your Startup" chapters in Part III, Teams for more info.
